# Efficiency Matrix Overview

The Efficiency Matrix is the optimization pillar of the Revona orchestration system.  
It tracks performance, identifies bottlenecks, and documents improvements across workflows, systems, and creative processes.

## Purpose of the Efficiency Matrix
The Efficiency Matrix exists to:
- measure system performance  
- identify friction points  
- track improvements over time  
- support data-informed refinement  
- ensure the studio becomes faster, smoother, and more effective  

This pillar turns intuition into measurable progress.

## What Lives Here
The Efficiency Matrix contains:
- **Performance Metrics** — time, complexity, throughput  
- **Bottleneck Analyses** — where friction occurs and why  
- **Improvement Logs** — changes made and their impact  
- **Optimization Experiments** — tests to increase efficiency  
- **System Health Reports** — periodic evaluations  

## Relationship to Other Pillars
- Blueprint defines the philosophy  
- Engine Room defines the execution  
- Field Notes captures insights  
- **Efficiency Matrix measures and improves performance**

This pillar ensures the system becomes more powerful over time.

## Living Optimization System
The Efficiency Matrix evolves as workflows and systems mature.  
It is updated regularly to reflect current performance and future goals.
