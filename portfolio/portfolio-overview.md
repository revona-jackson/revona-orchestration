# Portfolio Overview

The Portfolio pillar is the outward-facing expression of the Revona orchestration system.  
It contains case studies, project summaries, narrative framing, and documentation that showcases the studio’s work to external audiences.

## Purpose of the Portfolio
The Portfolio exists to:
- present work clearly and professionally  
- demonstrate creative and operational capability  
- document projects for clients, collaborators, or employers  
- support career growth and opportunities  
- translate internal systems into external value  

This pillar is the public face of the studio.

## What Lives Here
The Portfolio contains:
- **Case Studies** — deep dives into major projects  
- **Project Summaries** — concise overviews of completed work  
- **Narrative Frames** — how projects are positioned and explained  
- **Process Documentation** — behind-the-scenes insights  
- **Showcase Assets** — screenshots, exports, visuals  

## Relationship to Other Pillars
- Blueprint defines identity  
- Engine Room defines execution  
- Field Notes captures insights  
- Efficiency Matrix tracks performance  
- **Portfolio communicates the results**

This pillar turns internal excellence into external impact.

## Living Portfolio System
The Portfolio evolves as new projects are completed and new stories emerge.  
It is updated intentionally to reflect the studio’s best and most current work.
