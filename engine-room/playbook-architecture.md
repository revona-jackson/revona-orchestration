# Playbook Architecture

Playbooks are guided, narrative-style documents that walk the user through executing a workflow or system.  
They provide context, decision support, and real-world guidance that complements the structured logic of workflows and systems.

## Purpose of Playbooks
Playbooks exist to:
- make complex processes approachable  
- provide human-friendly guidance  
- support decision-making during execution  
- reduce cognitive load  
- ensure consistent outcomes across repeated runs  

A playbook is the “how-to guide” for running a workflow or system in practice.

## Playbook Structure
All playbooks follow a consistent structure:

1. **Purpose**  
   A clear explanation of what the playbook helps you accomplish.

2. **When to Use This Playbook**  
   The scenarios or triggers that indicate this playbook is relevant.

3. **Prerequisites**  
   What must be prepared or completed before starting.

4. **Step-by-Step Guidance**  
   A narrative walkthrough of the process.  
   Each step includes:
   - what to do  
   - why it matters  
   - what to watch for  
   - AI-human handoff notes (if relevant)

5. **Decision Points**  
   Key choices the user may encounter, with guidance for each path.

6. **Common Pitfalls**  
   Mistakes, misunderstandings, or failure modes to avoid.

7. **Completion Checklist**  
   A simple list confirming the playbook has been fully executed.

8. **Version Notes**  
   Updates, refinements, or improvements over time.

## Playbook Design Principles
All playbooks must align with:

- **Clarity** — written in plain, supportive language  
- **Guidance** — explains not just what to do, but why  
- **Contextual Awareness** — acknowledges real-world scenarios  
- **AI Collaboration** — identifies where AI assists or leads  
- **Consistency** — follows the design language and system logic  

## Playbook Categories
Playbooks fall into several categories:

- **Creative Playbooks** — writing sessions, ambience creation, editing  
- **Operational Playbooks** — publishing, scheduling, asset management  
- **Analytical Playbooks** — research, synthesis, optimization  
- **Portfolio Playbooks** — case study creation, cross-platform expansion  
- **System Playbooks** — guided execution of multi-layered systems  

Each category has its own folder and naming conventions.

## Living Playbook System
Playbooks evolve as workflows and systems mature.  
They are updated frequently to reflect new insights, tools, and best practices.

The playbook architecture ensures that every complex process in the system has a clear, supportive, human-friendly guide.
