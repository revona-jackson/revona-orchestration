# AI‑Native Design Philosophy

The Revona orchestration system is built with the assumption that AI is not an add‑on, but a core collaborator. This philosophy shapes how workflows are structured, how decisions are documented, and how creativity is scaled.

## Designed for Multi‑Agent Collaboration
Workflows assume the presence of multiple AI agents with different strengths.  
Tasks are structured so each agent can contribute where it excels.

## Human‑in‑the‑Loop by Default
AI handles pattern recognition, synthesis, and iteration.  
Humans provide direction, taste, and final judgment.  
The system is built around this partnership.

## Iteration as a Workflow Primitive
AI thrives on iteration.  
The system encourages rapid cycles of draft → refine → finalize, rather than single‑pass execution.

## Context‑Rich Inputs
Prompts, templates, and workflows are designed to give AI the context it needs to produce high‑quality output without micromanagement.

## Explainability and Traceability
Every AI‑assisted decision is documented with its rationale.  
This ensures the system remains transparent, auditable, and easy to evolve.

## Creativity at Scale
AI is used to expand creative capacity — generating variations, exploring alternatives, and accelerating ideation — while maintaining a coherent human‑driven vision.
