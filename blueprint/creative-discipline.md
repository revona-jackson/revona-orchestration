# Creative Discipline Framework

Creativity inside the Revona orchestration system is not chaotic or accidental — it is structured, intentional, and repeatable. This framework defines how ideas move from spark to execution without losing coherence or momentum.

## Structured Exploration
Creative work begins with guided exploration rather than open‑ended wandering.  
Prompts, constraints, and thematic anchors keep ideation focused.

## Defined Creative Channels
Ideas flow through specific channels — ambience, narrative, systems, workflows — so each concept has a clear home and purpose.

## Iterative Refinement
Drafts are expected.  
Revisions are expected.  
The system treats iteration as a natural part of the creative process, not a sign of failure.

## Constraint‑Driven Innovation
Boundaries are used to spark creativity rather than limit it.  
Constraints create clarity, reduce decision fatigue, and sharpen the final output.

## Documentation as a Creative Tool
Every idea, workflow, and decision is documented.  
This creates a creative memory — a system that remembers what works, what doesn’t, and why.

## Consistency Over Intensity
The system favors sustainable creative habits over bursts of inspiration.  
Small, consistent progress compounds into large‑scale creative output.
